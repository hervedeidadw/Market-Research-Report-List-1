<p><strong>The Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) Spectrom Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Far-infrared FTIR</li><li>Mid-infrared FTIR</li><li>Near-infrared FTIR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) spectrometry market is segmented into three types based on the infrared spectrum range. Far-infrared FTIR operates in the range of 10 to 1000 µm, useful for studying molecular vibrations and lattice dynamics in solid materials. Mid-infrared FTIR, covering 2.5 to 25 µm, is widely used for organic compounds' analysis, providing information on molecular structure. Near-infrared FTIR, spanning 0.75 to 2.5 µm, is typically employed in quantitative analysis and food quality assessment, focusing on overtones and combinations of molecular vibrations.</p></p>
