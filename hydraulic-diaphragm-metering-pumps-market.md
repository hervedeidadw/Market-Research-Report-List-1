<p><p>Hydraulic diaphragm metering pumps are categorized based on diaphragm materials, each suited for different applications. CSM (chlorosulfonated polyethylene) medium offers excellent chemical resistance and durability, ideal for harsh environments. FPM (fluoroelastomer) medium provides high-temperature tolerance and superior resistance to aggressive chemicals, making it suitable for demanding applications. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) medium is known for its exceptional non-stick properties and chemical compatibility, often used in critical processes. Other market types might include various specialty materials tailored for niche chemical processes.</p></p>
