<p><p>The osteoarthritis pain market consists of three main types of drug delivery: oral, injection, and external. Injectable treatments, such as corticosteroids and hyaluronic acid, provide targeted relief directly into the affected joint, often delivering faster effects. External treatments, like topical NSAIDs or capsaicin creams, are applied directly to the skin over the painful area, offering localized relief with minimal systemic effects. Each type caters to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
