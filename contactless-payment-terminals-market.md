<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>Near field communication</li><li>Bluetooth</li><li>Radio-frequency identification</li><li>Carrier-based mobile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contactless payment terminals enable quick transactions using various technologies. Infrared systems transmit data through light waves, requiring a direct line of sight. Near Field Communication (NFC) allows devices to communicate when within a few centimeters, making it popular for mobile payments. Bluetooth offers greater range, facilitating transactions between devices without physical proximity. Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) utilizes electromagnetic fields to transfer data, often used in access cards. Carrier-based mobile markets involve transactions via mobile network providers, linking payments through SIM cards.</p></p>
