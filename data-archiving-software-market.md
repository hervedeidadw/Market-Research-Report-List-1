<p><p>Data archiving software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based archiving enables organizations to store and manage data offsite, benefiting from scalability, lower upfront costs, and reduced maintenance requirements. This option allows for easy access and sharing across multiple locations. On-premises archiving, however, involves installing software within the organization's infrastructure, offering greater control over data security and compliance. It is ideal for businesses with stringent regulatory requirements or those preferring in-house management.</p></p>
