<p><p>Fluoride ion selective electrodes (ISEs) are critical in various applications, primarily in water quality monitoring and environmental assessments. The market can be categorized into liquid membrane and PVC membrane types. Liquid membrane electrodes use a solvent-based layer that selectively responds to fluoride ions, offering high sensitivity and fast response times. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes incorporate polyvinyl chloride matrices, providing durability and ease of use. Both types are vital for reliable fluoride detection in diverse settings, ensuring compliance with health standards.</p></p>
