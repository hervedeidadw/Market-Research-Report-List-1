<p><p>The Low Extremities Fixation Product market comprises various internal and external fixation methods for treating fractures in the lower limbs. Internal fixation includes plates, screws, nails, and other specialized devices that stabilize bone alignment during healing. Plates distribute load and provide support; screws secure fragments; and nails are inserted into the medullary canal for stability. External fixation involves external devices connected to the bone, offering support while allowing for swelling and access to the injury. These techniques are crucial for effective fracture management.</p></p>
