<p><p>Perfluorooctanesulfonate (PFOS) is a synthetic chemical compound belonging to the group of perfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS), known for its remarkable resistance to heat, water, and oil. Historically used in various industrial applications, including stain repellents, surfactants, and firefighting foams, PFOS has garnered attention due to its environmental persistence and potential health risks.
