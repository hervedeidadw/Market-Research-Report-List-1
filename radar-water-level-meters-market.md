<p><p>Radar water level meters are classified into low frequency and high frequency types based on their operational frequencies. Low frequency radar operates typically below 10 GHz, making it suitable for measuring levels in turbulent or foamy conditions and in larger bodies of water, offering greater penetration. High frequency radar, usually above 10 GHz, provides higher resolution measurements, making it ideal for precise level measurement in applications with smaller water bodies or less turbulence, thus enhancing performance and accuracy in diverse environments.</p></p>
