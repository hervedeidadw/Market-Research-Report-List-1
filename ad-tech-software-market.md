<p><p>The Ad Tech Software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ad tech allows users to access and manage advertising tools via the internet, offering scalability, ease of installation, and reduced maintenance costs. On-premises solutions, in contrast, are installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data and customization options, but often requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance.
