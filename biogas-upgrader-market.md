<p><p>The biogas upgrader market consists of various technologies used to enhance the quality of biogas by removing impurities and increasing methane content. Membrane separation involves using selective membranes to separate methane from carbon dioxide and other gases. Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) relies on sorbents that adsorb impurities under varying pressure conditions, thereby enriching methane. Other methods may include chemical scrubbing and cryogenic processes. Each technology offers distinct advantages, influencing efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and application suitability in biogas upgrading.</p></p>
