<p><p>DC power connectors are essential components used for power supply in various electronic devices. The market features three primary types: surface mount connectors, designed for mounting directly onto printed circuit boards for compact designs; panel mount connectors, which are installed onto the exterior of enclosures for easy access; and through-hole connectors, which pass through the PCB, providing robust mechanical support. Each type serves specific applications, influencing factors like space constraints, accessibility, and reliability in electrical connections.</p></p>
