<p><p>The telemedicine technology market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based telemedicine platforms allow healthcare providers to access and share patient data remotely, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced infrastructure costs. These solutions are increasingly popular due to ease of use and integration with other digital systems. Conversely, on-premise solutions involve software installed locally within a healthcare facility, offering greater control over data security and compliance, but often requiring higher upfront investment and maintenance efforts.</p></p>
