<p><p>The kidney dialysis equipment and supplies market comprises two primary types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis involves using a machine to filter waste and excess fluids from the blood, typically performed in clinics or at home with portable devices. In contrast, peritoneal dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdomen as a natural filter, allowing patients to perform the procedure at home with dialysis solutions. Both methods cater to the growing prevalence of chronic kidney diseases and require specialized equipment and supplies.</p></p>
