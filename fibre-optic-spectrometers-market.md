<p><p>The fiber optic spectrometer market can be categorized into three primary types: LED-based spectrometers, UV-VIS-NIR spectrometers, and laser-based spectrometers. LED spectrometers utilize light-emitting diodes for a broad spectral range, making them suitable for various applications. UV-VIS-NIR spectrometers cover ultraviolet, visible, and near-infrared ranges, providing versatility in material analysis. Laser spectrometers utilize focused laser light, offering high sensitivity and resolution for precise measurements in applications like environmental monitoring and biomedical analysis. Each type serves distinct needs across industries.</p></p>
