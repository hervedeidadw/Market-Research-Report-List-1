<p><ul><li>Shaft Seal Diaphragm Structure</li><li>Flexible Tube Structure</li><li>Torsional Tube Structure</li><li>Differential Pressure Target Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Target flowmeters are crucial in measuring fluid flow rates, utilizing various structural designs. The shaft seal diaphragm structure offers robust sealing for high-pressure applications. Flexible tube structures provide versatility and ease in installation, making them suitable for diverse environments. Torsional tube structures enhance measurement accuracy by utilizing the twist of the tube under fluid flow. Differential pressure target structures measure flow by assessing the pressure difference across a restriction, ensuring precise readings in varying conditions. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
