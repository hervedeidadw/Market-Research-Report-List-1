<p><ul><li>Manufacturing Process:Float</li><li>Manufacturing Process:Rolled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The translucent glass market is primarily categorized by its manufacturing processes: float and rolled. Float glass is produced by floating molten glass on a bed of molten tin, resulting in a smooth, uniform surface ideal for high-quality applications. In contrast, rolled translucent glass is created by passing glass through rollers, imparting texture and thickness variations, often used for decorative purposes and privacy features. Each process offers distinct characteristics, catering to diverse architectural and design needs in the construction and interiors sectors.</p></p>
