<p><ul><li>Donor Antenna</li><li>Indoor Antenna</li><li>Signal Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellular repeater market encompasses several types: Donor Antennas, Indoor Antennas, and Signal Amplifiers. Donor Antennas capture signals from nearby cell towers to enhance reception. Indoor Antennas distribute the boosted signals throughout a building, ensuring comprehensive coverage. Signal Amplifiers strengthen weak signals before redistribution, improving communication quality and reliability. Together, these components create a seamless network experience, addressing coverage challenges in urban and rural areas by enhancing cellular connectivity for users across various environments.</p></p>
