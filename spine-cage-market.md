<p><p>The spine cage market is segmented into four main types: lumbar, cervical, thoracolumbar, and thoracic. Lumbar cages are designed for the lower back, providing support and stability during spinal fusion. Cervical cages target the neck region, facilitating recovery from disc or vertebral issues. Thoracolumbar cages cater to the thoracic and lumbar junction, addressing complex spinal disorders. Lastly, thoracic cages are used for the upper and mid-back, focusing on spinal stability and alignment, essential for effective surgical outcomes and patient rehabilitation.</p></p>
