<p><strong>The Infrared Proximity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Proximity Sensor market comprises two main types: active and passive sensors. Active sensors emit infrared light and detect its reflection from nearby objects, allowing for precise distance measurement and object detection. They're often used in applications like automatic doors and robotic systems. In contrast, passive sensors rely on detecting infrared radiation emitted by objects within their field, commonly used in safety systems and environmental monitoring. Both types serve essential roles in automation and various industrial applications.</p></p>
