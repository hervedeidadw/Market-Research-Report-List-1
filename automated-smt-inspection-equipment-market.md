<p><p>Automated SMT Inspection Equipment encompasses various technologies essential for ensuring quality in surface-mount technology. Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) Machines gauge the accuracy of solder paste application prior to component placement, identifying potential defects early in the process. Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Machines utilize advanced imaging to detect errors on assembled PCBs, ensuring proper component placement and soldering. Other inspection equipment may include X-ray machines for hidden solder joints or automated functional testers, enhancing the overall reliability and efficiency of electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
