<p><p>Bipolar electrosurgical devices are medical instruments used in various surgical procedures to cut tissue and coagulate blood while minimizing damage to surrounding structures. Unlike monopolar devices, bipolar systems utilize two electrodes located in close proximity, allowing for more precise control and reduced thermal spread.
