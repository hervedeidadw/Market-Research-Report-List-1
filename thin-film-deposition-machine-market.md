<p><p>The thin-film deposition machine market encompasses various technologies used for creating thin films on substrates. PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment utilizes physical methods, such as evaporation or sputtering, to deposit films. CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment relies on chemical reactions to form films from gaseous precursors. ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition) equipment deposits films one atomic layer at a time, allowing for precise control of thickness and composition. Each method offers unique advantages for applications in electronics, optics, and coatings.</p></p>
