<p><ul><li>Nozzle Flapper Valve</li><li>Jet Action Valve</li><li>Direct Drive Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-stage electro-hydraulic servo valve market features three primary types: the nozzle flapper valve, jet action valve, and direct drive valve. The nozzle flapper valve utilizes a flapper that controls fluid flow through nozzles for precise movements. The jet action valve employs high-speed jets to produce rapid response times, making it suitable for dynamic applications. The direct drive valve eliminates mechanical linkage, providing direct control over fluid flow with high accuracy and rapid response, catering to advanced control systems in various industries.</p></p>
