<p><p>Automated Vertical Lift Systems (AVLS) can be categorized into two primary market types: Single Level Delivery and Dual Level Delivery. Single Level Delivery systems transport goods to a designated floor without stacking, optimizing space efficiently on one level. In contrast, Dual Level Delivery systems allow for multiple levels of storage and retrieval, enhancing throughput and storage capacity. These systems are widely used in warehouses and manufacturing environments, improving operational efficiency and reducing manual handling in inventory management.</p></p>
