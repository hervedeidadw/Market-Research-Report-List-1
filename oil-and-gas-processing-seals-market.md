<p><p>The oil and gas processing seals market comprises two primary types: single seals and double seals. Single seals consist of one sealing element that prevents leakage, suited for low-pressure applications. In contrast, double seals consist of two seals arranged in tandem, providing an additional layer of protection against leakage, ideal for high-pressure and hazardous environments. The choice between them depends on factors like application requirements, pressure levels, and safety standards, ensuring operational efficiency and environmental protection in the industry.</p></p>
