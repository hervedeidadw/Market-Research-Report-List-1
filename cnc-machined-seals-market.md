<p><p>The CNC machined seals market encompasses various types tailored for different applications. Piston seals prevent fluid leakage in hydraulic cylinders, while rod seals ensure a tight fit for moving rods. Rotary seals are designed for rotating shafts, minimizing friction and wear. Guide rings help align components, and backup rings provide additional sealing support. Gaskets create leak-proof connections between surfaces, while other specialty seals cater to unique operational requirements. Each type is crucial for maintaining efficiency and performance in machinery.</p></p>
