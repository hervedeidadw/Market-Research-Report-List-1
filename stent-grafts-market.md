<p><p>The stent grafts market is primarily divided into two types: AAA stent grafts and TAA stent grafts. AAA stent grafts are designed to treat abdominal aortic aneurysms, reinforcing the weakened artery wall and preventing rupture. In contrast, TAA stent grafts are used for thoracic aortic aneurysms, providing support and stability to the thoracic aorta. Both types enhance patient outcomes through minimally invasive procedures, reducing recovery times and complications associated with traditional surgical interventions, leading to increased adoption in vascular surgeries.</p></p>
