<p><p>Nanoindentation and scratch test instruments are advanced tools used to evaluate the mechanical properties of materials at the nanometer scale. Nanoindentation measures hardness and elastic modulus by pressing a sharp indenter into the material surface, providing insights into material behavior under small loads. Scratch tests assess scratch resistance and adhesion by sliding an indenter across the material, which helps in determining durability and wear characteristics.</p><p>The market for nanoindentation and scratch test instruments is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in industries such as electronics, automotive, and materials science. The need for precise characterization of materials in research and quality control applications is pushing advancements in these testing technologies.
