<p><strong>The Racing Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT4</li><li>DOT5</li><li>DOT5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The racing brake fluid market features several key types: DOT4, DOT5, and DOT5.1. DOT4 is a glycol-based fluid known for high boiling points suitable for performance applications, while DOT5 is silicone-based and offers superior moisture resistance, but is not compatible with glycol fluids. DOT5.1 combines high performance with compatibility, providing high boiling points and moisture absorption features similar to DOT4. Each type serves different racing needs, balancing performance, safety, and system compatibility for optimal braking performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
