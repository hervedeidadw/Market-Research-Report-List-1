<p><p>The Surface Haptics market encompasses three primary types of technologies. Electrotactile technologies use electrical stimulation to create tactile sensations on surfaces, enhancing user interaction. Thermal technologies employ temperature variations to convey feedback, adding a sensory dimension to touch experiences. Mechanical feedback technologies utilize physical movements or vibrations to simulate touch, effectively communicating information through surface dynamics. Together, these technologies aim to improve user engagement and enhance experiences in applications such as virtual reality, gaming, and touchscreen interfaces.</p></p>
