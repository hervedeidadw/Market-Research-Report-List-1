<p><p>The photochromic lenses market features various types based on material and index. Glass photochromic lenses offer high optical clarity and scratch resistance but are heavier. Standard plastic lenses (1.53 coat and uncoat) are lightweight and affordable with decent performance. Mid-index plastic lenses (1.53 to 1.65) balance thickness and weight for better aesthetics. High-index plastic lenses (above 1.65) are the thinnest, ideal for strong prescriptions. Polycarbonate lenses, known for durability and impact resistance, are commonly used in eyewear for active lifestyles.</p></p>
