<p><p>The UHF inlays and tags market is divided into two primary types: UHF inlays and UHF tags. UHF inlays are typically embedded within labels or stickers, designed for various applications such as inventory management and supply chain tracking. UHF tags, on the other hand, are standalone RFID devices that can be attached directly to products, assets, or containers, enabling streamlined identification and data collection. Both types leverage ultra-high frequency technology for efficient wireless communication over longer distances.</p></p>
