<p><p>The pool timer market is divided into two main types: programmable and non-programmable timers. Programmable pool timers allow users to set specific schedules for pool equipment, offering flexibility and energy savings by automating operations based on preferred timings. Non-programmable timers, on the other hand, typically have a simple on/off switch and require manual adjustment each time, making them less versatile.
